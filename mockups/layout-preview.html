Notes on the phone layout

  • Each flag is a single row, opponent's cards on the left, yours on the right.
  • The flag column scrolls vertically — you swipe down to see flags 1 through 9.
  • Your hand is pinned at the bottom and scrolls horizontally if it overflows.
  • Opponent's face-down hand sits as a thin strip at the top.
  • Deck + discard sit just above your hand.
  • Claim button sits below each flag row when claimable.

4. Pass-the-device screen

Shows full-screen between turns. The board is hidden behind it so the wrong player can't peek. Tap "Ready" to reveal your hand.
